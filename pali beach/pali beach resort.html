PALI BEACH RESORT



Ideal for fun and relaxation, Pali Beach Resort is located in the Mira Bhayanda area of Mumbai. The hotel is not too far from the city center: just away, and it normally takes about 70 minutes to reach the airport. A well-kempt environment and its proximity to Uttan Virgin Beach, Vasai Fort, Essel World give to this hotel a special charm. Pali Beach Resort offers impeccable service and all the essential amenities to invigorate travelers. 24-hour room service, 24-hour front desk, car park, room service, airport transfer are just a few of the facilities that set Pali Beach Resort apart from other hotels in the city.
